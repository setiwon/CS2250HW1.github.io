The King: Kento Momota

Kento Momota is the most decorated badminton player to hail from Japan.
He is known for his precise shot selection, court control, defense, and unrivaled netplay.
Momota entered into Guinness Book of World Records for "The most badminton men's singles titles in a season",
for his achievements by winning 11 titles in the 2019 season.
Unfortunately, he was involved in a fatal car crash in 2020 that killed the driver and Momota was seriously injured.
In addition, COVID-19 and further injuries have resulted in him underperforming and not in form.
However, the King will recover and return to reclaim his throne.
